About Maschinell

Maschinell Industrial Enterprises is a trusted provider of industrial electrical and automation solutions, delivering reliable products and services across diverse industrial sectors.

As an Authorized Distributor of Schneider Electric, we provide genuine, high-quality electrical products backed by global standards of safety, performance, and innovation.

Our expertise includes electrical panels, switchgear systems, industrial lighting, automation solutions, power distribution equipment, and hydraulic systems tailored to modern industrial requirements.

Leadership

Driven by innovation, engineering excellence, and long-term industrial partnerships.

Sumit Saboo

Director

Sumit Saboo is an experienced industrial entrepreneur leading Maschinell Industrial Enterprises, with a strong background in engineering solutions, project execution, and business development. He holds an M.Tech in Power Electronics and Drives from VIT University, Vellore, and a B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering & Technology. With roots in a reputed Marwari business community and an entrepreneurial family background, he combines traditional business values with a modern, technology-driven approach to deliver sustainable growth, operational excellence, and enduring client partnerships.

Sabhya Saboo

Chief Operating Officer

Sabhya Saboo holds a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and brings extensive experience in managing operations across multiple organizations in the electrical and healthcare domains. Coming from a strong business community background, with early exposure to business management, she combines practical insight with technical expertise to drive efficiency, ensure quality execution, and contribute to the sustained growth of Maschinell Industrial Enterprises.
