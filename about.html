About Maschinell

Maschinell Industrial Enterprises is a trusted provider of industrial electrical and automation solutions, delivering reliable products and services across diverse industrial sectors.

As an Authorized Distributor of Schneider Electric, we provide genuine, high-quality electrical products backed by global standards of safety, performance, and innovation.

Our expertise includes electrical panels, switchgear systems, industrial lighting, automation solutions, power distribution equipment, and hydraulic systems tailored to modern industrial requirements.

We serve industries including manufacturing, infrastructure, construction, power & energy, commercial facilities, and industrial automation with a strong focus on quality, efficiency, and long-term reliability.
