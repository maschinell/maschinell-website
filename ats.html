Frequently Asked Questions

A Schneider Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) automatically transfers electrical loads between the primary utility power source and a backup generator during a power failure. This ensures uninterrupted power supply for critical electrical systems and equipment.

A Schneider ATS performs source transfer automatically without human intervention, whereas a manual changeover switch requires an operator to switch between utility and generator power sources manually.

Schneider TransferPacT and TransferPacT Active ATS solutions are widely used for generator backup applications in commercial, industrial and critical infrastructure projects. The ideal solution depends on system size, load requirements and monitoring needs.

Schneider ATS solutions are commonly installed in hospitals, data centers, industrial plants, airports, commercial buildings, hotels and other facilities where uninterrupted power supply is critical.

Schneider TransferPacT ATS solutions provide fast source transfer, reliable operation, intelligent monitoring and enhanced electrical safety, making them ideal for critical power distribution and business continuity applications.
