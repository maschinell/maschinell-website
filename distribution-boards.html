Frequently Asked Questions

A Schneider Distribution Board (DB) is an electrical panel used to safely distribute power across multiple circuits while protecting connected equipment. Distribution boards improve electrical safety, simplify circuit management and support efficient power distribution in residential, commercial and industrial installations.

Schneider Electric offers Acti9 Isobar P, Acti9 Isobar B, Easy9 Distribution Boards and PrismaSeT solutions. The ideal choice depends on the number of circuits, load requirements, installation environment and future expansion needs.

A Distribution Board distributes electrical power to multiple outgoing circuits, while a Panel Board is a broader term that may include control, protection and monitoring equipment. Distribution Boards are commonly used for organized and safe power distribution.

Schneider Distribution Boards are widely used in residential buildings, commercial offices, hospitals, educational institutions, industrial facilities and infrastructure projects where reliable electrical distribution is required.

Schneider Acti9 Isobar and PrismaSeT Distribution Boards offer modular design, enhanced safety, reliable power distribution, easy maintenance and compliance with international electrical standards, making them ideal for modern electrical installations.
