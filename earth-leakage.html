Frequently Asked Questions

Schneider Earth Leakage Protection solutions are designed to detect leakage currents and earth faults in electrical systems. They help prevent electric shock, reduce fire risks and improve the safety and reliability of residential, commercial and industrial installations.

An RCCB provides direct residual current protection and disconnects the circuit during leakage faults, while an Earth Leakage Relay works with external breakers to monitor leakage currents and provide advanced protection for larger electrical systems.

Schneider Electric offers Vigi Modules, Acti9 Vigi Devices, Vigirex Earth Leakage Relays and Earth Leakage Monitoring Systems. The ideal solution depends on system size, protection requirements and application needs.
