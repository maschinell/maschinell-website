Electrical Panels

Maschinell Industrial Enterprises, an authorized Schneider Electric distributor in Jaipur, provides high-quality industrial electrical panels including PCC panels, MCC panels, APFC panels, control panels and customized power distribution solutions.

PCC Panel

Power Control Center (PCC) panels are used for efficient distribution and control of electrical power in industrial and commercial environments.

Key Features:
  • Reliable power distribution
  • Overload & short circuit protection
  • Robust and durable design
  • Easy maintenance
Applications:
  • Manufacturing plants
  • Industrial units
  • Commercial buildings
MCC Panel

Motor Control Center panels are designed to control and monitor multiple motors efficiently in industrial systems.

Key Features:
  • Centralized motor control
  • Enhanced safety mechanisms
  • Compact and modular design
Applications:
  • Factories
  • Processing plants
  • Automation systems
APFC Panel

Automatic Power Factor Correction panels improve power efficiency and reduce electricity costs.

Key Features:
  • Automatic capacitor switching
  • Energy saving system
  • Improves power factor
Applications:
  • Industrial plants
  • Commercial complexes
VFD Panel

Variable Frequency Drive panels are used to control motor speed and improve energy efficiency.

Key Features:
  • Smooth motor control
  • Energy efficient operation
  • Reduces wear & tear
Applications:
  • Pumps
  • Fans
  • Industrial automation
