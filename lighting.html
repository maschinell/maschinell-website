Lighting Solutions

Maschinell Industrial Enterprises provides advanced industrial and commercial lighting solutions for manufacturing facilities, warehouses, infrastructure projects and commercial buildings. As an authorized Schneider Electric distributor in Jaipur, we offer energy-efficient lighting systems, flameproof lights, high bay lighting and customized illumination solutions designed to improve safety, productivity and operational efficiency.

Flame Proof Lights

Engineered for hazardous industrial environments, these lights ensure safe operation in areas exposed to gases, vapors, and combustible dust while maintaining reliable illumination.

High Bay Lights

Ideal for large industrial spaces such as warehouses and manufacturing units, high bay lights provide uniform illumination at elevated heights.

Flood Lights

High-intensity lighting solutions designed for wide-area outdoor illumination in industrial sites, construction zones, and infrastructure projects.

Street Lights & Poles

Durable and energy-efficient lighting systems for roads, industrial zones, and public infrastructure ensuring safety and visibility.

High Mast Lighting

Designed for large open areas such as highways, ports, and industrial yards, providing high-intensity illumination from elevated mounting systems.

Applications

Industrial Plants
