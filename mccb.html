Frequently Asked Questions

The selection of an MCCB depends on factors such as rated current, breaking capacity,voltage level,and application requirements. Schneider Electric offers EasyPact CVS, Compact NSX, Compact NS and EasyPact EZC series for different industrial and commercial applications.

EasyPact CVS is designed as a reliable and economical protection solution, while Compact NSX offers advanced protection features, electronic trip units, communication capabilities and energy monitoring functions for critical installations.

The lifespan of an MCCB depends on operating conditions, switching frequency, ambient temperature, maintenance practices and the electrical load connected to the circuit.

Schneider Electric MCCBs are known for their reliability, safety, international compliance standards, wide product range and suitability for diverse industrial and commercial applications.

Advanced series such as Compact NSX can support energy monitoring, communication modules and intelligent protection functions for modern electrical distribution systems.
