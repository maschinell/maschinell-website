Frequently Asked Questions

Schneider RCCB (Residual Current Circuit Breaker) and RCD (Residual Current Device) are designed to detect earth leakage currents and disconnect the power supply automatically. They help protect people from electric shock and improve electrical safety in residential, commercial and industrial installations.

An MCB protects electrical circuits against overloads and short circuits, while an RCCB protects against earth leakage currents and electric shock hazards. Both devices are often used together for complete electrical protection.

Schneider Electric offers Acti9 iID RCCB, Easy9 RCCB and other residual current protection solutions. The ideal choice depends on current rating, sensitivity requirements and installation type.

Schneider RCCB and RCD devices are available in various sensitivity ratings including 30mA, 100mA and 300mA. The appropriate selection depends on personnel protection, equipment protection and application requirements.

Schneider Acti9 iID RCCB and Easy9 RCCB devices offer reliable earth leakage protection, enhanced electrical safety, compliance with international standards and long-term operational reliability for modern electrical installations.
