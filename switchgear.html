Switchgear Solutions

Maschinell Industrial Enterprises, an authorized Schneider Electric distributor in Jaipur, offers a comprehensive range of Schneider Electric switchgear solutions including MCCB, ACB, MCB, RCCB, ATS, switch disconnectors and surge protection devices. Designed for industrial, commercial and infrastructure applications, our switchgear solutions provide reliable protection, safe power distribution and enhanced operational efficiency.

Air Circuit Breakers (ACB)

High-capacity breakers used for protection of electrical circuits in industrial power systems.

Key Features:
  • High breaking capacity
  • Reliable protection
  • Long operational life
Applications:
  • Power distribution panels
  • Industrial plants
Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB)

Used for protecting low voltage circuits from overload and short circuits.

Key Features:
  • Compact design
  • Fast tripping mechanism
Applications:
  • Residential systems
  • Commercial installations
Motor Protection Circuit Breaker (MPCB)

Provides reliable protection for motors against overload and electrical faults.

Key Features:
  • Motor-specific protection
  • Compact and efficient
Applications:
  • Motor control panels
  • Industrial machinery
Moulded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCB)
